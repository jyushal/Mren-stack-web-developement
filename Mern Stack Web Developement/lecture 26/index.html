Gulab Jamun

Ingradients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Take required quantity of sugar and water in a deep pan.
  2. Stir and bring to boil on high flame for sugar to dissolve.
  3. Now add milk and cardamom seeds to the sugar water.
  4. Boil further and strain.
  5. Then add saffron and cardamom powder.
  6. bring the mixture to rolling boil for about 5-6 minutes
  7. Hot and tasty sweet dish is ready to serve.
